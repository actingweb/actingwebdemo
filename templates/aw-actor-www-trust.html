About Trust Relationships

Trust relationships allow actors to securely share data and communicate with each other. Each relationship has a type and approval status.

Status Indicators

  • Approved - You've approved this relationship
  • Pending - Awaiting your approval
  • Verified - Cryptographically verified

Relationship Types

  • friend - Full access to public data
  • associate - Limited access
  • acquaintance - Basic access only
